Frequently Asked Questions

After completing your purchase, you will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours containing your digital membership card. Simply show this at the admissions desk on your first visit.

Supporter tier and above includes one complimentary guest pass per visit. A joint family membership upgrade is available for an additional $40/year — contact us at members@monomuse.org for details.

MonoMuse is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. The portion of your membership fee that exceeds the fair market value of benefits received may be tax-deductible. Please consult a tax advisor for your specific situation.

Donations are one-time or recurring contributions with no attached benefits. They go directly to our general fund to support programming, acquisitions, and community outreach. Memberships include a package of visitor perks in return for your annual fee.
